1. The Louvre, Paris, France - The Louvre is the world's largest art museum and a historic monument in Paris, France. It houses some of the most famous works of art in the world, including the Mona Lisa and Venus de Milo.

2. The Metropolitan Museum of Art, New York City, USA - The Metropolitan Museum of Art is one of the world's largest and most visited art museums. It houses over two million works from around the world spanning 5,000 years of history.

3. Prado Museum, Madrid, Spain - The Prado Museum is one of the most important art museums in Europe and houses a vast collection of Spanish and European art from the 12th to 19th centuries.

4. Uffizi Gallery, Florence, Italy - The Uffizi Gallery is one of Italy's oldest and most important art galleries and houses some of the greatest works by Italian Renaissance masters such as Botticelli and Michelangelo.

5. National Gallery, London, UK - The National Gallery in London is home to an impressive collection of Western European paintings from the 13th to 19th centuries including works by Rembrandt and Van Gogh. 

6. Rijksmuseum, Amsterdam, Netherlands - The Rijksmuseum is a Dutch national museum dedicated to arts and history located in Amsterdam. It houses a large collection of Dutch Golden Age paintings as well as other works from around Europe from various periods in history. 

7. Musee d'Orsay, Paris, France - The Musee d'Orsay is a museum located on the left bank of Paris that houses French artwork from 1848 to 1914 including Impressionist masterpieces by Monet and Renoir among others. 

8. State Hermitage Museum, St Petersburg Russia - The State Hermitage Museum is one of Russia's oldest museums located in St Petersburg that contains over three million items ranging from ancient Egyptian artifacts to modern European paintings by Matisse and Picasso among others. 

9. Vatican Museums, Vatican City - Located within Vatican City are several museums that house some of the world's greatest works including Michelangelo's Sistine Chapel ceiling frescoes as well as sculptures by Bernini among many others from various periods in history up until modern times. 

10 .Museo del Prado , Madrid , Spain-The Museo del Prado is an art museum located in Madrid that contains some 8600 paintings dating back to 12th century up until 19th century with masterpieces by Goya , Velazquez , El Greco among many others .
