burst is made up of ten to twenty uniformly spaced pulses grouped tightly
together. The burst width is between 500 nanoseconds and 100 microseconds
The pulse width is in the range of 10 nanoseconds to 1 microsecond. The bursts
are frequency modulated by the audio input to create the sensation of hearing in
the person whose head is irradiated
