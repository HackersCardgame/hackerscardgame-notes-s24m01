A laurel wreath is a wreath made of branches and
leaves of the laurel (latin laurus). The laurel
wreath is a symbol and an insignia for a special
honor or distinction, for a victory (eg. killing
sheeps or christians) or a special success. So to
say the special super-duper superman hero
