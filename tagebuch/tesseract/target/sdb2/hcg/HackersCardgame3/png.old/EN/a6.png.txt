Inventor Year
The inventor of the programming language Smalltalk
was the first who used objects in software
development, that's the reason why it is called "object
riented" Programming Language. Gutenberg and
the letters of his printing presses was one of the first
example in of Templates in this context. A class is a
single template for part of a complete software, e.g.
"Name, address, phone number" would be a template
for a person in an address database.
