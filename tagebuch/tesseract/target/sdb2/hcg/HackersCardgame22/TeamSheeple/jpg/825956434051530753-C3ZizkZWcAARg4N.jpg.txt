Activation-Times

Inventor Petron Corporation (1933) Year 1952
An analogue continuous tape, where one can
record suggestions such as e.g. 2 + 2 = 5, which
is automatically activated at certain times. The
tape will automatically turn off after 5 minutes.
With digital Equipment (computer / mobile phone)
one can realize more complex systems.
