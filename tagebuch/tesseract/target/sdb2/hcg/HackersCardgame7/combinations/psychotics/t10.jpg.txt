Activation-Times

Inventor Petron Corporation (1933) Year 1952

An analogue continuous tape, where one can
record suggestions such as e.g. 2 + 2 = 5, which
is automatically activated at certain times.
The tape will automatically turn off after 5
minutes. With digital Equipment (computer /
mobile phone) one can realize more complex
systems. with cards: t7, t3, t12

Use Cases

During the night: learning while sleeping, learn
foreign languages, unfortunately you can also
upload a complete (wrong) world view to the Brain
