The inventor of the programming language
Smalltalk was the first who used objects in
software development, that's the reason why it
is called "object oriented" Programming
Language. Gutenberg and the letters of his
printing presses was another example. A class
is a single template for part of a complete
software, e.g. "Name, address, location,
phone, ..." would be a template for a person
in an address database.

Anwendungsfalle
Simplification/standardization through "pre-
printed (software) templates"
